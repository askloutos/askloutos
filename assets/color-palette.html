Dark sections

There is no dark mode — there are dark sections.

#0F1419 is used for the signup band and footer only. Text on it: cream (--text-inverse) for headings, #8B98A5 (--text-inverse-muted) for support copy, crimson for the CTA. Inputs use --color-dark-2 (#1A2129).
